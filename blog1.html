How to Build a Portfolio Website

Your portfolio is your digital handshake. In this post, we’ll explore how to build a personal site that reflects your skills, personality, and goals.

Start with a clean layout, use consistent fonts, and highlight your best work. Tools like GitHub Pages, Netlify, and Figma can help you design and deploy quickly.

Don’t forget to include a contact form, social links, and a blog to share your journey!
